Explore Our Rivers

Adventure Awaits on the Water

Main Fork of the Salmon River, Idaho

Nestled deep in the Frank Church Wilderness of No Return, the Salmon River winds through breathtaking landscapes. Known as the "River of No Return," visitors fall in love with its rugged beauty, lush pine forests, and stunning wildlife.

Desolation and Gray Canyon, Utah

Located in central Utah, the Green River flows through the stunning Desolation and Gray Canyons. This 84-mile trip offers towering cliffs, exciting rapids, and ancient petroglyphs, making it an unforgettable experience.

Grand Canyon, Arizona

The ultimate river adventure-rafting through the Grand Canyon is a world-class experience. From thrilling rapids to ancient ruins and hidden waterfalls, this journey through Arizona's iconic landscape is unforgettable.
