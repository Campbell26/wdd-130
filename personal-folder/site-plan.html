Home page

Whether you're looking to learn new techniques, explore pristine rivers, or simply enjoy the beauty of nature, River Run Guides has something for everyone.

We prioritize your safety and satisfaction, offering top-notch equipment and personalized instruction. Guided Trips & Services

River Run Guides offers customized guided fly fishing trips to some of the most pristine rivers and streams. Whether you’re looking for a half-day excursion or a multi-day adventure, we have the perfect trip for you.

Half-Day & Full-Day Trips

These trips are perfect for anglers looking to get out on the water without an overnight commitment.

  • Half-Day Trip: 4-hour guided fishing experience with gear included.
  • Full-Day Trip: 8-hour guided trip, including lunch and fly selection recommendations.

Multi-Day Adventures

For those wanting a true wilderness experience, our multi-day excursions allow you to explore hidden fishing spots, camp by the river, and truly disconnect. These trips include:

  • Overnight camping accommodations
  • All meals provided
  • Customized instruction on fly selection, casting, and reading the water

Family & Group Packages

Looking for an activity the whole family can enjoy? Our family-friendly trips make fishing fun for everyone, with simplified instruction for beginners and kids.

About Us

River Run Guides was founded in 2005 by a group of passionate anglers who wanted to share their love for fly fishing with others. Over the years, we have grown into a leading guide service, known for our expertise, commitment to conservation, and dedication to customer satisfaction. Our team of experienced guides brings a wealth of knowledge and a friendly, approachable demeanor to every trip.

Our mission is simple: to provide exceptional fly fishing experiences that connect people with nature and foster a love for the sport. We believe in sustainable practices and work closely with local conservation organizations to protect the rivers and streams we fish. A portion of every trip fee goes towards these efforts, ensuring that future generations can enjoy the same pristine environments.

Meet our team of expert guides, each with their own unique story and passion for fly fishing. From former professional anglers to lifelong outdoor enthusiasts, our guides are the heart and soul of River Run Guides. They are dedicated to making your trip safe, enjoyable, and educational, sharing tips and techniques that will enhance your skills and deepen your appreciation for the sport.

At River Run Guides, we are more than just a guide service; we are a community of anglers who share a love for the outdoors. We invite you to join us on the river and experience the magic of fly fishing for yourself. Whether you’re a seasoned pro or a first-time fisher, we promise an adventure you’ll never forget.
